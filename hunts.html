01 · Fully Outfitted Hunts

One guide. One hunter. An animal already found.

Our fully guided hunts put you shoulder to shoulder with someone who has scouted the country and knows where the animals are living. Rifle or archery, spot and stalk or water holes, we build the day around your tag.

Every guided hunt includes

  • Pre-season scouting & field planning
  • Professional guide services
  • Lodging for your hunt dates
  • Airport pickup & drop-off
  • Breakfast & sack lunches
  • Dinner at local restaurants
  • Field care & meat preparation
  • Local knowledge you keep for life

Processing fees are the hunter's responsibility. Tags and licenses are drawn or purchased through Wyoming Game & Fish. Ask us about the draw.

03 · DIY Access Hunts

Our ground. Your hunt. On your own two feet.

For the hunter who wants to run their own show. Pay an access fee, follow the ranch rules, and hunt animals we have already located across private and public land.

How access works

  • Fully guided hunts always have first priority on our units.
  • DIY spots are first come, first served, timestamped in the order received.
  • If a hunt is oversubscribed, spots are drawn by random lottery.
  • Dates are staggered to limit the number of hunters per area at a time.
  • Applications typically open in July and August each year.

We operate on BLM, State of Wyoming, and the Medicine Bow-Routt and Black Hills National Forests.

Preference Point Program

Play the Wyoming draw the smart way.

Wyoming is a draw state and points are a long game. Let us manage yours. We track your points, watch every deadline, and send you annual updates so you never miss a window.

Preference Point Hunt Program

Points that pay you back

Build points toward a future guided hunt with us and we discount 100% of your point cost off the hunt price. Buy elk points for ten years before you draw, and that is a $520 credit toward your hunt. Youth who draw later as adults earn adult rates on every point.

Maximum discount $500 per hunt. Discounts apply to points bought through our program.

Preference Point Only Program

Just keep me in the game

Not ready to book, but want to keep building? We will purchase and manage your points for a flat $5 per point on top of the Wyoming Game & Fish fees, and keep you posted on every deadline.
