Price notes & add-ons

  • All guided hunts include meals. 5% state sales tax applies to camp lodging and meals ($2.50/day).
  • Lodging tax varies by the county your hunt lodging is in.
  • A 3% public-land use fee applies in areas operating on public land.
  • Upgrade cow elk & antelope to a 1×1 guide ratio for $250/day.
  • Bring an observer for $200/day.
  • Additional days on request: deer & antelope $750/day, elk $950/day.

What's included

  • Pre-season scouting
  • Guide services
  • Lodging
  • Airport pickup & drop-off
  • Breakfast & sack lunches
  • Dinner at local restaurants
  • Field care & meat prep
  • Local knowledge

Not included: Wyoming licenses and tags, which are drawn or purchased through Wyoming Game & Fish, and meat processing.
