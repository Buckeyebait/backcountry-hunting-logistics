Our Story

Everyone deserves the hunt of a lifetime.

Everything Backcountry Hunting Logistics does reinforces one belief: everyone deserves the hunt of a lifetime, and there is no better place for it than central Wyoming. Our customers are everyday people who want to make a dream real, one hunt at a time.

We believe anyone can have a successful Wyoming hunt in the beautiful backcountry with a little direction and a lot of desire. Our hunts are affordable, and they include giving you all the knowledge you need to harvest the largest trophy possible.

We operate across several units and focus on quality Wyoming hunts, following herd management and conservation. Our goal is to make memorable hunts while keeping them sustainable for the generations behind us. Our company quotas for each unit support conservation, so we run limited hunts through every one of our operating areas.

Ask any western big game hunter, and 95% of them will tell you scouting was the key to their success. We make it our business to know the country and the animals before you arrive, so every day you spend with us counts.

Conservation

Limited hunts, on purpose.

We hold our own quotas on every unit we operate. That means fewer hunters, less pressure, and more mature animals, season after season. It is how we protect the herds we love and keep these hunts here for the generations that follow. A hunt with BHL is a vote for the future of Wyoming's backcountry.
